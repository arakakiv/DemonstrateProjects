Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it Open Source?

Yes, and you're allowed to contribute! Look at our repository and all that our license allows.

How can I contribute?

Open an Issue in the official repository on github. Don't be shy!

What are keys?

They are access tokens that must be passed on the request body to you and other people get all your projects.

How to get/allow other people to get my projects?

It's quite simple! Just do a POST request to our API! In the body, you must pass your key. If you wanna other people to access your projects, you'll need to share your key. In the headers, you must set Content-Type header to application/json. If you wanna a single project, use as query parameter the index parameter.
